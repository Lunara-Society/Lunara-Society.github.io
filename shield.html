Your Business.
Verified. Accountable. Found.

The Shield is a status you earn through verification, not a badge you buy. A reviewer checks your business registration and domain before anything goes live — and certification can be revoked if standards are later violated.

Check if your business is in the registry
How verification actually works

01
You submit an application
Business name, website, domain, legal registration number, and contact email. Free to apply — no payment required to be reviewed.
02
Identity verification
Your legal business registration is checked against official records before anything is marked verified.
03
Domain verification
We confirm you actually control the domain you're applying under, via DNS or a verified email at that domain.
04
Human review — no automatic approval
A reviewer checks the evidence and signs their name to the decision. Applications are not approved automatically, by an AI, or on payment.
05
Decision: approved, rejected, or sent back for more evidence
Only approved applications receive a public Lunara ID and a registry entry. Rejections can be appealed with additional evidence.
06
Public, checkable registry entry
Anyone — a customer, a journalist, another AI system — can look up your status directly. Status shown is always the true current status.
07
Ongoing accountability
Certification can be reviewed and revoked if standards are later violated. Permanent and unaccountable are not the same thing — ours is accountable.
