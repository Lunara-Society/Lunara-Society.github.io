The Standard

Seven pillars of constitutional governance.

Certification is assessed against the seven pillars of the Lunara Constitution. Each pillar has defined criteria, a verification mechanism, and a corresponding record in the public Shield registry.

I
Verified Identity

Legal entity identity is formally established and publicly accountable.

II
Declared Governance Framework

The operational rules governing the AI system are documented and accessible.

III
Transparency of Contact

Any interaction initiated by an AI system discloses its institutional origin.

IV
Data Sovereignty

Data handling policies are explicit, limited, and subject to accountability.

V
Audit Accessibility

Governance records are accessible to legitimate counterparties and regulators.

VI
Revocability

Certification can be suspended or revoked. Revocation is logged publicly.

VII
Proportionality

Governance requirements scale proportionately to the risk profile of deployment.
