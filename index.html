The Lunara Protocol

Five pillars.
One standard.

The Lunara architecture is not a product. It is an institutional framework — built to govern the intersection of autonomous intelligence and legitimate commerce.

Shield
Verified trust membership for businesses. A declaration of openness to qualified AI engagement — on their terms, under their sovereignty.
Signal
The intelligence layer. Real-time market signals that inform routing decisions, reputation scoring, and match quality across the network.
Registry
A curated, machine-readable catalog of verified entities. Businesses and agencies that meet the Lunara standard — discoverable, trusted, ranked.
Gateway
The structured API layer. External AI systems connect to the Lunara network through authenticated, schema-validated endpoints.
Reputation
Interaction quality builds a portable trust record. Performance compounds into routing priority. The network rewards those who earn it.
